### Project 4: E Commerce Sales Dashboard

This project presents an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Dashboard Design techniques. The dashboard provides key business insights into sales performance, product performance, customer behavior, payment methods, and marketing channels.

## Objectives

The goal of this dashboard is to help stakeholders:

 - Monitor overall business performance
 - Track revenue trends over time
 - Identify top-performing products
 - Analyze order fulfillment status
 - Evaluate payment methods
 - Measure marketing channel effectiveness
 - Monitor coupon usage and customer purchasing behavior
   
### Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Dashboard Design
  
### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
 - Total Revenue: $1,264,761.96
 - Total Quantity Sold: 3,535 Units
 - Total Orders
 - Average Order Value

## Dashboard Features
# Revenue Analysis
  - Monthly Revenue Trend
  - Revenue by Quarter
    
# Product Analysis
  - Revenue by Product
  - Quantity Sold by Product

# Order Analysis
 - Order Status Distribution
 - Coupon Usage Analysis
   
# Payment Analysis
- Revenue by Payment Method
  
# Marketing Analysis
 - Revenue by Referral Source
   
## Key Insights
# Revenue Performance
 - The business generated total revenue of $1.26M during the period under review.
 - Revenue fluctuated across months, indicating seasonal sales patterns.
# Product Performance
 - Chairs and Printers generated the highest revenue.
 - Chairs recorded the highest quantity sold, indicating strong customer demand.
# Order Fulfillment
 - Delivered orders represented the largest share of total orders.
 - Cancelled and returned orders highlight areas for operational improvement.
# Payment Methods
 - Credit Card and Online payments contributed the highest revenue.
 - Digital payment methods were preferred by customers.
## Referral Sources
- Referral and Facebook channels generated the highest revenue.
- Marketing investments can be focused on high-performing channels.
## Coupon Performance
- Coupon campaigns influenced a significant portion of customer purchases.
- Promotional offers can be optimized to improve sales conversion.

## Business Value

This dashboard enables management to make data-driven decisions by providing clear visibility into sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and marketing effectiveness.
